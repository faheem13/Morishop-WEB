How to Delete Your Bingo Seller Account

Step-by-Step Instructions

  1. Log in to your account in the Bingo Seller mobile app.
  2. Navigate to Store from the bottom menu.
  3. Select Account Settings.
  4. Tap Delete Account.
  5. Confirm your action and enter your credentials when prompted.

Your user account and all associated data will then be deleted.

Data Deleted

  • Username
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Address
  • KYC documents
  • Payment data

Please note: We may retain your data for up to 30 days in our database for security and compliance reasons, after which it will be fully purged.
