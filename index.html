Explore Products

Discover a wide range of products on the TAHA home page, from newly added items to discounted deals and top-selling products. Easily browse categories, find trending products, compare prices, and enjoy a simple shopping experience designed for Mauritius.

TAHA brings buyers and local vendors together in one easy and fun platform.

Find Nearby Products

Discover products available around you with TAHA's Nearby page. Browse products from local vendors using an intuitive swipe-based experience designed to make finding products fast, engaging, and effortless.

Simply swipe through nearby products, explore what is available in your area, and instantly view product details, pricing, and store information. Whether you're looking for fashion, electronics, food, services, or daily essentials, TAHA helps you find what you need closer to home.

Found something you like? Get directions to the seller's location, contact the vendor directly, and visit the store with ease. Supporting local businesses has never been easier.

Scroll Through Reels

Discover products through engaging short-form videos designed to help you explore items in a more interactive and visual way. The Reels page allows buyers to swipe vertically through product videos, making shopping more enjoyable and informative.

Vendors can showcase their products, demonstrate features, highlight specifications, and share real-world usage examples through short videos. Whether it's fashion, electronics, beauty products, food, home décor, or services, buyers can quickly understand what makes each product unique.

Inspired by modern video discovery platforms, TAHA Reels combines entertainment and shopping into a seamless experience, helping buyers discover trending products while giving vendors a powerful way to showcase their products and reach new customers.

Checkout From Cart

Add multiple products from the same vendor to your cart and complete your purchase in a single checkout. TAHA makes shopping easier by allowing buyers to combine products from a seller into one order, reducing the need for multiple transactions and helping save on delivery costs.

Select your preferred delivery method, pickup option, or shipping address, then complete your order securely. Buyers can purchase products from multiple vendors while maintaining separate carts and checkouts for each seller, ensuring accurate delivery and order management.

Whether you're buying a single item or multiple products from your favourite vendor, TAHA's cart experience helps streamline the purchasing process and provides a smooth shopping journey from product discovery to checkout.

Track Order Status

Stay informed every step of the way with TAHA's order tracking system. Buyers can monitor the progress of their orders in real-time, providing complete visibility from purchase to delivery or collection.

Once an order is placed, its status is automatically updated as it moves through different stages such as payment verification, order processing, preparation, dispatch, pickup readiness, and completion. This allows buyers to know exactly where their order stands at any moment.

Receive clear updates directly within the app and view detailed information about each order, including purchased items, delivery method, payment status, and estimated progress. No more uncertainty about whether an order has been confirmed or shipped.

TAHA helps create a transparent shopping experience by keeping buyers and vendors connected throughout the entire order journey, ensuring greater confidence and peace of mind when shopping online.
