By using the App, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

When you register or use our services, we may collect:

  • Name

b) Account Information

You may sign in using:

  • Email and password

  • Facebook account

We may receive basic profile information from these providers, such as name, email address, and profile picture.

c) Device and Usage Information

We may collect:

  • Device type and operating system version

d) Location Information

With your permission, we may collect your device location to show nearby products and vendors and improve the delivery and service experience.

2. Permissions We Use

Camera

We use camera permission to allow users to take photos, such as profile pictures, product images, or chat images.

Gallery / Storage Access

We use gallery or storage access to allow users to select and upload images from their device.

Location Services

We use location services to show nearby products and vendors.

Notifications

We use notifications to send order updates, messages, promotions, and alerts.

You can enable or disable these permissions at any time from your device settings.

We use your information to:

Users may communicate with vendors through in-app chat, including sending text and images.

Messages and shared media may be stored to enable communication between users and vendors. Users should avoid sharing sensitive personal information unnecessarily.
